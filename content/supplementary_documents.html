We are now able to publish press summaries for judgments on the Find Case Law service. They should be uploaded in the normal way, via TDR. We are currently unable to publish supplementary documents on the Find Case Law service.

If you have any other forms of supplementary document that go with a judgment/decision you are uploading, please email them to judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk. They will be saved on our internal system and can be distributed to re-publishers upon request.
