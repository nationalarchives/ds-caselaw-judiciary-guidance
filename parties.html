Parties and labels formatting

Alignment

When setting the placement of the parties, it is important to not use the tab function. The parser cannot ascertain where each element should be when tab is used.

Before uploading, we recommend looking at the judgment in web view before uploading. Sometimes, the alignment of the parties look right in print view but actually be askew when looking web view.

Example of incorrectly formatted parties

Shankar v Genpact (UK) Limited & Ors. [2022] EAT 184

Party name labels

Web view can be used to check the placement of labels, to ensure they are in the right position.

Similarly to aligning the parties, do not use the tab button to position the party labels as the parser cannot recognize where they are supposed to be.

If party labels are on the same line as the party name, issues can occur if the label is styled or aligned differently as the parser struggles to differentiate between the two sections.
