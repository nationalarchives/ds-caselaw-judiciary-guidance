If you are experiencing any difficulties in obtaining an NCN, please contact the Judgments Helpdesk team: judgmentshelpdesk@judiciary.uk

Documents without an NCN

We currently publish a limited number of decisions which do not contain an NCN. If you believe your judgment/decision does not require an NCN, please contact us: judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk
