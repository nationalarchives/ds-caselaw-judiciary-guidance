Tables in judgments or decisions should only be used for tabular data within the body of the judgment. They should not be used on the front page of a judgment or decision.

Regarding the body of a judgment or decision, we have made several improvements to how we handle tables and table formatting in Word. Issues tend to arise when images are used in tables. For more information on this, please go to the Images section.

For help with the guidance on this page please email us at judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk
