Helping you to prepare documents for The National Archives

This site aims to help support you when preparing your court judgments and tribunal decisions ahead of uploading them to the Find Case Law service.

We have included guidance and advice to help you to ensure that your documents are faithfully formatted against your original; as well as being accessible when they appear online.
